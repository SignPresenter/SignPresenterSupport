# Affiliate & Commissions

<div className="article-intro">

The Affiliate page is where accounts enrolled in the SignPresenter affiliate program track their referrals, see commissions accruing, and review past commission checks. If you've referred new accounts to SignPresenter (or you publish [public feeds](./feeds/)), this is your earnings dashboard.

</div>

:::info
This page is visible only to accounts that have an affiliate setting configured. If you don't see **Affiliate** in your sidebar, your account isn't enrolled in the program.
:::

Open it from **Settings → Affiliate**.

## Your affiliate links

The top of the page shows your unique affiliate links:

- A general SignPresenter link with your code embedded
- A church-specific landing page link with the same code

Share these to refer new accounts. When someone signs up via your link, they're tagged with your code and any subscription revenue they generate counts toward your commission.

## Current Affiliates

The left column lists every affiliate code on your account (most accounts have one, but some have multiple for different campaigns). Each row shows:

- The code itself
- The commission percentage you earn on referred subscriptions
- The percentage you earn on subscriptions to feeds you own (the "owned feed commission")

Click into any affiliate to see a detailed breakdown of every referred account and the commissions that have accrued.

## Commission Checks

The right column shows your **Commission Checks** — the periodic payouts SignPresenter has issued to you. Each check is a batch of commissions covering a set of invoices. Click any check to drill into its line-items.

When SignPresenter cuts a new check, eligible commissions are bundled and the total is paid out — typically monthly, but the exact cadence depends on your agreement.

## Current Commissions

Below the checks, the **Current Commissions** panel shows accruals that haven't yet been included in a check — money you've earned but haven't been paid yet. This rolls into the next commission check when SignPresenter processes payouts.

Commissions are split by affiliate code so you can see which campaigns are performing.
