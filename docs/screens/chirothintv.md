# ChiroThinTV Setup

<div className="article-intro">

ChiroThinTV is a chiropractic-focused content feed for SignPresenter. This guide takes you from a fresh ChiroThinTV account to a paired screen running professional clinic content in under 10 minutes.

## Step 1: Create your ChiroThinTV account

1. Go to [ChiroThinTV.com](https://chirothintv.com).
2. Click **Start My Free Trial** or **Register**.
3. Fill out the registration form.
4. Log into your new account.

## Step 2: Set up your screen in SignPresenter

1. Go to **Step 3: Screens**.
2. Click **First Screen**.
3. Name your screen (for example, "Reception Area").
4. Click **Save**.

## Step 3: Install SignPresenter on your Fire Stick

### Device requirements

SignPresenter works on Fire Sticks running **Fire OS** (Android-based) and Android devices running **Android 10.0 or newer**.

**Recommended:** [Fire TV Stick HD](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0CQMRKRV5) — best for 24/7 signage thanks to auto-reboot after power loss.

:::warning
Not every Fire Stick is compatible. See the [device compatibility guide](./compatible-devices) before buying.
:::

### Install steps

1. Set up your Fire Stick: plug in, sign into your Amazon account, complete software updates.
2. Hold the microphone button on the remote and say "Sign Presenter".
3. Select the SignPresenter app and click **Download**.
4. Open SignPresenter from your home screen.

## Step 4: Pair your device

1. SignPresenter shows a **4-digit code** on the TV.
2. Back on [SignPresenter.com](https://signpresenter.com), click **Pair TV** in the Screens section.
3. Enter the 4-digit code.
4. Click **Pair TV**.
5. On the Fire Stick, click **Run Schedule**.

## Step 5: Add billing information

After clicking Run Schedule, you'll be prompted to add billing.

1. Go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Enter your credit card information.
3. Click **Update**.

:::note
Billing doesn't start for 30 days, and you can cancel any subscription at any time.
:::

Your ChiroThinTV feed will now run on your screen.

## Optional: advanced features

### Manual playlist for patient education

This slideshow lets you walk new patients through pricing and information manually.

1. **Playlists → Horizontal 16x9**.
2. Find **ChiroThin Manual Playlist**.
3. Name it (for example, "Patient Pricing").
4. Choose your preferred pricing points.
5. Click **Save**.

**Using the manual playlist on the Fire Stick:**

1. Press **Back** on the remote.
2. Select the **Manual Playlist**.
3. Use left/right arrows to navigate slides.
4. Press **Up** to see all slides at once.
5. Press **Back** and select **Run Schedule** to return to the regular feed.

### Add social media promotion

1. **Playlists → Horizontal 16x9**.
2. Find **Social Media Promotion**.
3. Click **Subscribe → Create Playlist**.
4. Name your playlist.
5. Enter your Facebook, Twitter, or Instagram handles.
6. Click **Save**.

### Add Google / Yelp reviews requests

1. **Playlists → Horizontal 16x9**.
2. Find **Reviews → Subscribe → Create Playlist**.
3. Name it "Reviews" and click **Save**.

This displays prompts asking viewers to leave Google or Yelp reviews.

### Upload your own images

1. Click **Messages → Upload**.
2. Upload your images.
3. Create a category and a name. Videos and other message types are also included in your subscription.

## Step 6: Add playlists to your screen

1. Go to **Step 3: Screens** and click your screen.
2. Select **24/7** for the schedule.
3. Adjust playlist weights — they must sum to 100%:
   - ChiroThin Auto Playlist: 50%
   - Reviews: 5%
   - Social Media: 5%
   - Other playlists: remaining percentage
4. Click **Save**.

The screen will update within 1–2 minutes to show all playlists at the configured weights.
