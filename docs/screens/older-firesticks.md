# Older Fire Sticks

<div className="article-intro">

Older Fire Sticks (the ones that ask you to log in instead of pairing with a 4-digit code) run a legacy version of the SignPresenter app. They still work, but they struggle with some videos. Here's how to tell, and what to do about it.

</div>

## How to tell if you have an older Fire Stick

You'll know you have a legacy device if SignPresenter requires you to **log in** instead of pairing the Fire Stick from the website with a 4-digit code.

The biggest symptom is that some videos will fail to play smoothly, or you'll see a screen like this:

![Older Fire Stick error](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/193816328-5d29c2fd-6a47-4aa0-81d2-73173a46134a.jpeg)

## Recommended fix: upgrade the Stick

The cleanest solution is to replace the device with a current-generation [Fire TV Stick HD](https://amzn.to/3letCr1) — about $25 and fully compatible.

## Workaround: lower the resolution

If you can't upgrade right away, you can reduce the issue (not eliminate it) by lowering the Stick's output resolution.

1. Press **Home** on the Fire Stick remote to exit SignPresenter.

   ![Home screen](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/193817623-40354c43-440f-477b-86e5-f3b865d83aab.png)

2. Open **Settings**.

   ![Settings](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/193817726-17db8fa8-4bc4-4c56-ac61-059a14b5af35.png)

3. Open **Display & Sounds**.

   ![Display & Sounds](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/193817765-b20afd89-233c-406b-a2a8-4853ad9bcb87.png)

4. Open **Display**.

   ![Display](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/193817793-7ba2f2dc-22f9-4c1b-a00e-704fe57f727f.png)

5. Open **Video Resolution**.

   ![Video Resolution](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/193817826-98a71c1c-1aef-4503-adfa-32d5b0afd958.png)

6. Pick a lower resolution (720p instead of 1080p, for example).

   ![Pick resolution](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/193817846-153ccba3-2e7c-4400-94bd-f1a5bbcdee6a.png)

7. Confirm — the screen will flash briefly and apply the change.

   ![Confirm](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/193817879-c11de370-64af-481b-9209-e70098a790c7.png)
