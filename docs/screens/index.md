## Pairing a TV

The pairing flow is covered in detail in [Initial Setup → Step 3](../getting-started/initial-setup#step-3-connect-your-screen). Quick version:

1. Install the SignPresenter app on your Fire TV or Android device.
2. Open the app — you'll see a 4-digit code.
3. In your account, go to **Step 3: Screens**, select the screen, click **Pair TV**, and enter the code.
4. On the device, choose **Run Schedule**.

## Recommended hardware

For most users, the **[Fire TV Stick HD](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0CQMRKRV5)** ($24.99) is the sweet spot — affordable, auto-reboots after power loss, plenty fast for 1080p. See [Compatible Devices](./compatible-devices) for the full breakdown.
