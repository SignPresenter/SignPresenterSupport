# Vertical Screen Setup

<div className="article-intro">

Setting up a vertical (portrait) screen takes less than 30 seconds. Two settings — rotation and playlist — and a quick app restart.

</div>

## Step 1: Change the screen rotation

1. Go to the **Screens** tab (Step 3).
2. Select **First Screen** from the left sidebar.
3. Find the **Rotation** dropdown.
4. Select **90 — Vertical** (or **270 — Vertical** depending on which way the TV is mounted).
5. Click **Save**.

## Step 2: Switch to a vertical playlist

1. Scroll down to the **Playlist** section.
2. Select your vertical (9:16) playlist from the dropdown.
3. Remove any horizontal playlists if needed.
4. Click **Save**.

## Step 3: Reload the app on your device

For the rotation change to take effect:

1. On your Fire Stick or Android device, press the **Home** button.
2. Open the SignPresenter app again.
3. Your screen now displays in vertical mode.

## Rotation options

- **0 — Horizontal** — Standard landscape
- **90 — Vertical** — Portrait, rotated 90° clockwise
- **180 — Horizontal** — Upside-down landscape
- **270 — Vertical** — Portrait, rotated 90° counter-clockwise

:::note
After changing rotation settings, you must close and reopen the SignPresenter app for changes to take effect.
:::
