# Connect Lessons.church to SignPresenter

<div className="article-intro">

Lessons.church is a free, open-source curriculum platform — and SignPresenter pulls its scheduled lessons in automatically. Plan an entire quarter (or year) of children's ministry content in under 30 minutes, then let SignPresenter handle weekly delivery.

</div>

## Why connect Lessons.church?

1. **Set it once.** Lessons you schedule on Lessons.church auto-fill into the matching SignPresenter playlist. Your week-to-week setup is preconfigured.
2. **Mix in anything.** Combine Lessons.church curriculum with other automatic feeds (Digital Felt, Big Big Worship, Yancy's music) inside SignPresenter — the entire week's content auto-updates.
3. **It just works.** Stuck? Call us at [918-994-2638](tel:9189942638) and we'll help you finish setup.

## Step 1: Create a Lessons.church account

1. Go to [Lessons.Church](https://lessons.church/) and click **Login**.

   ![Login](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/202710373-d5a7054c-99a5-4642-956d-1b5885182b74.png)

2. Click **Register**.

   ![Register](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/202710531-a6be5ac1-fadc-4a45-b5ec-2531e30dc6e7.png)

3. Enter your information and click **Register**.

   ![Account info](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/202710697-74ff478f-f38f-407a-aff4-98b871441d83.png)

4. Check your email and click **Set Password**.

   ![Email link](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/202710747-ff0db5d3-e242-498e-a8d9-2cb3ef543ebe.png)

5. Set a password and click **Login**.

   ![Set password](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/202710934-533e74e1-eb5e-4a91-b6f8-aa321bcf66b8.png)

6. Pick your church (or create a profile if you don't have one).

   ![Church info](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/202711100-7d8ed003-ab3d-4888-8575-45a62abda670.png)

7. From the launcher page, click **Lessons.church**.

   ![Choose Lessons](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/202711277-61c8dced-a604-48ad-b23b-79ae8229aa41.png)

8. You're in.

   ![Lessons landing](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/202711363-ec4c302f-0cd3-4ba3-b72f-b09cbaa0d638.png)

## Step 2: Add a classroom and schedule

1. Click **Schedules**.

   ![Schedules](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/202711525-746ce835-6188-46bd-8255-f9b5c228fee0.png)

2. Add a classroom.

   ![Add classroom](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/202711671-4711e591-d55c-46d4-9e84-2f496e1638e6.png)

3. Click the classroom you just created.

   ![Open classroom](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/202742762-1c60d3c2-9657-40dd-880c-0b0666e2ecce.png)

4. Click **Add a Schedule**.

   ![Add schedule](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/202742912-e043c618-cca0-4e6b-b7dc-362bd0e103fe.png)

5. Fill in the date, **Program**, **Study**, **Lesson**, and **Venue**, then click **Save**.

   ![Fill schedule](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/202743181-b11b3b64-1146-4980-9eec-4b3a90651352.png)

## Step 3: Subscribe and copy the playlist URL

1. Click **Subscribe**.

   ![Subscribe](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/202743312-970388ae-15f1-4ec4-afb2-38a131e38c09.png)

2. Click the URL link to reveal it.

   ![Reveal URL](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/202743535-6f79f57b-b507-42d8-a0f6-0c93a7fc343d.png)

3. Copy the URL.

   ![Copy URL](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/202743777-377226ba-986e-4902-9dfa-e182c54c54e7.png)

## Step 4: Add the feed in SignPresenter

1. Open SignPresenter and click **Playlist**.

   ![SignPresenter playlist](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/202743947-6adf460d-f3fb-4ff0-9b8b-f3b8b2ebce47.png)

2. Click **Horizontal**.

   ![Horizontal](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/202744745-2adbd7b3-b7ca-47b2-bf40-7b7f68927936.png)

3. Click **External Feed**.

   ![External feed](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/202744837-93d20702-c3d8-4a78-a2a9-cc5eef81131c.png)

4. Name the playlist and paste the URL you copied. Click **Save**.

   ![Name and paste](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/202745027-8fe169b7-2d59-4992-90c9-7f550f14b04d.png)

## Step 5: Build a wrapper playlist (optional but recommended)

This is what lets you mix the curriculum with your own worship music or announcements.

1. Make a second horizontal playlist.

   ![New horizontal](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/202745237-e005889f-adcd-4a79-a802-1f02d5800a5d.png)

2. Use **Create a New Custom Playlist** at the top.

   ![Create custom](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/202745360-3378d925-6fbb-44d7-a0ff-35a4953ee813.png)

3. On the right, click **Playlist**.

   ![Right panel](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/202745448-d25f122d-9bab-4e02-b9f2-53aee74c2eb9.png)

4. Find the External Feed playlist you made in Step 4.

   ![Find feed](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/202745554-f81dff0f-22d4-41b8-90fe-dedf313add8e.png)

5. Click the gear icon to customize a section — useful for inserting your own slides between curriculum slides.

   ![Customize](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/202745741-d8a67106-575e-4e81-aa69-4c1608cde89b.png)

6. Set the playlist to play only certain slides — for example, skip the verse slide every week.

   ![Set stop points 1](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/202746246-40374979-ae17-43c0-aebb-2f25024c270d.png)
   ![Set stop points 2](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/202746349-9d37b98c-50d7-4bcc-b5ad-c1a3b47f2690.png)

7. Add the same external feed again, this time playing only slides 5–8.

   ![Add again](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/202746117-0d455af8-27db-41d9-926e-6b0f806acadf.png)

8. Click **Save**.

## Step 6: Add the playlist to your screen

1. Click **Screens**.

   ![Screens](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/202746562-a9e641fc-dce6-4537-9888-500e107b37d2.png)

2. Set the screen to **Both** mode (digital signage + slideshow).

   ![Both modes](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/202746749-c1ef938d-90e0-4cdd-88af-1082a6efa5c8.png)

3. Pick the correct playlist for the **Manual Playlist** dropdown.

   ![Manual playlist](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/202747484-3ba11e48-c403-4584-b0c0-10ee600ccb0f.png)

4. Scroll down to the schedule and pick the same playlist for the **Scheduled Playlist** dropdown.

   ![Scheduled playlist](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/202747504-620db6a7-f9ea-4f09-990c-9d5b18695529.png)

5. Click **Save** and test in your classroom.

   ![Save and test](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/202747704-5663bf7f-2c8e-4dda-b19a-eda468f51f55.png)
