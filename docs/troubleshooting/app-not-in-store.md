# Can't Find the App on Google Play / Apple TV / Roku

<div className="article-intro">

SignPresenter supports specific platforms — Apple TV and Roku are not currently supported. Here's the full list, and what to do if you can't find SignPresenter on a supported device.

</div>

## Supported platforms

### Amazon Fire TV devices

SignPresenter works on **Amazon Fire Sticks** and **Fire TVs** running **Fire OS** (Android-based).

**Recommended:** [Fire TV Stick HD](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0CQMRKRV5) — the most affordable option with auto-reboot capability, perfect for 24/7 signage.

### Android devices

SignPresenter supports Android devices running **Android 10.0 or newer**. [Download from the Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.signpresenter.screen&hl=en_US).

Budget-friendly Android options:

- [$19.88 onn. Android TV 4K UHD Streaming Device](https://www.walmart.com/ip/onn-Google-TV-4K-Streaming-Box-New-2023-4K-UHD-Resolution/2835618394)
- [$34.88 onn. Google TV 4K Plus Streaming Device](https://www.walmart.com/ip/ONN-4K-PLUS/15557424949)

### Built-in Fire TVs

TVs with built-in **Fire OS** work out of the box.

## Not supported

- **Apple TV** — not currently available
- **Roku** — not currently available
- **Fire TV Stick 4K Select** — runs Vega OS (not Fire OS) and is not compatible. See the [device compatibility guide](../screens/compatible-devices) for details.

## Quick compatibility test

If you can find **SignPresenter** in your device's app store, it will work. If you can't, the device probably isn't compatible — check the [Compatible Devices](../screens/compatible-devices) guide for what to buy instead.
