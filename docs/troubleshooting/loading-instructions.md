# Stuck on "Loading Instructions…"

<div className="article-intro">

If SignPresenter is stuck on a screen that says **Loading Instructions…**, it's almost always an IP-address conflict on the Fire Stick. The fix is a quick reboot.

</div>

![Loading Instructions screen](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/222148556-4d73ea6b-08b8-44d6-9784-911744cbe807.png)

## Quick fix: unplug and plug back in

The fastest way to reboot a Fire Stick is to pull the power cable, wait a few seconds, and plug it back in. Once it boots, SignPresenter should resume normally.

## Reboot via the menu

If unplugging isn't easy:

1. Press **Home** on the Fire Stick remote.
2. Open **Settings**.

   ![Settings](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/222148668-16405602-852c-4b88-aa64-b743df36abc7.png)

3. Navigate to **My FireTV** and press the center button.

   ![My FireTV](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/222148979-fc4f321d-947a-4249-9fff-3252df4a5784.png)

4. Select **Restart**.

   ![Restart](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/222149158-2d58bc24-015d-4753-be5b-2765f839f85e.png)

5. Confirm by selecting **Restart** once more.

   ![Confirm](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/222149763-9e10a763-738c-4136-b642-7c229d1f42f1.png)

## After the reboot

The Fire Stick should restart and launch SignPresenter automatically. If SignPresenter doesn't launch within 2 minutes, find the app on the home screen and select it.

## Still stuck?

If a reboot doesn't help:

1. Check your internet connection — SignPresenter needs WiFi to fetch instructions.
2. Try [clearing the cache](./clear-cache).
3. Open a [support ticket](./) and we'll dig in.
