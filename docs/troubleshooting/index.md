# Troubleshooting

<div className="article-intro">

Most SignPresenter issues are quick fixes. Pick the one that matches what you're seeing — and if nothing here helps, open a support ticket and we'll get on it.

</div>

## Common issues

- **[Stuck on "Loading Instructions…"](./loading-instructions)** — IP conflict; reboot the Fire Stick
- **[Videos won't preview in my browser](./cant-preview-videos)** — usually a Chrome autoplay restriction; click the popup to dismiss
- **[I can't find the app on Google Play / Roku / Apple TV](./app-not-in-store)** — supported platforms and what to do if it's missing
- **[I'm outside the USA](./outside-usa)** — change your Amazon Fire TV country to install regional apps
- **[Clear cache](./clear-cache)** — when files didn't download cleanly
- **[Disable "Still Watching?" pop-up](./still-watching)** — kill the Fire OS inactivity timeout

## Open a support ticket

When the fixes above don't help:

1. Make sure you're logged into your account.
2. Click **Settings**.

   ![Click settings](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/153202781-fe570571-6203-4c94-b081-fa8220a57440.png)

3. Click **Support**.

   ![Click support](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/153202994-7145fbe1-88af-4acd-8c0f-55712f7ffc83.png)

4. Click **New**.

   ![Click new](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/153203127-7b4f0907-4486-430a-b1aa-0aa5dc96b4cd.png)

5. Type the issue in the **Comments** box and click **Post**.

   ![Post comment](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/153203639-e10e6ff1-3307-42da-8641-922d362b1f20.png)

6. Need to attach a file? Click the ticket you just made.

   ![Click ticket](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/153203967-86a14296-0d90-4c0e-bd25-46a308f2a07f.png)

7. Upload a file or add another comment, then click **Post**.

   ![Upload file](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/153204258-4e582b0e-3845-4a41-ad16-f49909096d4b.png)

We'll get back to you as soon as possible with a fix.
