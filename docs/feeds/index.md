# Subscribing to Feeds

<div className="article-intro">

A **feed** is a curated content channel published by a content provider. Subscribe to a feed and its messages automatically appear in your account, ready to add to playlists. Feeds are how you get prebuilt content like weather, trivia, ChiroThinTV, Lessons.church, and provider-specific channels.

</div>

:::info
Only **account owners** can subscribe to or cancel feeds (because subscriptions affect billing). Other users can use the content once it's subscribed.
:::

## How feeds work

There are two flavors:

- **Playlist mode** — the feed publisher curates entire playlists. You add the feed playlist to a screen and the publisher manages the content from then on.
- **Library mode** — the feed publishes individual messages. You pick which ones you want and arrange them in your own playlists.

Most feeds also include **subscriber answers** — small bits of customization the publisher exposes to you (your zip code for a weather feed, your social handles for a reviews feed, your church name for a sermon feed).

## Subscribing

1. Open **Settings → Feeds**.
2. Browse the catalog and click a feed for details.
3. Click **Subscribe**.
4. Fill in any subscriber answers (zip code, business name, etc.).
5. Confirm — the feed's content is now available in your **Available Content** panel when building a playlist.

Most feeds include a free trial. Billing for a feed begins after the trial period ends and is billed alongside your normal SignPresenter subscription.

## Cancelling a feed

1. Open **Settings → Feeds**.
2. Find the feed you're subscribed to.
3. Click **Unsubscribe**.

Cancelling immediately stops new content from arriving. Existing playlist references continue to play whatever was last cached until you remove them from the playlist.
