# Update Your Billing Information

<div className="article-intro">

Change your credit card or billing details from inside your SignPresenter account in under a minute.

</div>

:::info
Only **account owners** can update billing.
:::

## Steps

1. Click your name in the top-right corner of SignPresenter.

   ![Click your name](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/151073993-f4f55c75-9298-4b2a-af5f-91a644628a23.png)

2. Click **Settings**.

   ![Settings](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/151074137-f7a8c11d-de9c-45ed-9e47-babd3dd67920.png)

3. On the left side of the Settings page, click **Billing**.

   ![Billing](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/151074374-8b71f069-a793-4fd5-b8c7-9a0fbafd25ce.png)

4. Enter your credit card information and click **Update**.

   ![Update billing](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/151074643-2d9c81ba-e285-43fd-b779-40170fb56e7d.png)

If SignPresenter is currently showing a billing-needed message on your screens, restart the SignPresenter app on each Fire Stick once you've updated billing — they'll pick up the new payment status.
