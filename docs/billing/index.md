# Billing & Payment

<div className="article-intro">

SignPresenter is **$10/month per screen**, with a 30-day free trial and no contracts. Add screens as you need them, cancel any time. This section covers everything billing-related.

</div>

:::info
Only **account owners** can change billing or manage subscriptions. Other users won't see this section in their account.
:::

## What's included

For $10 per screen per month you get:

- Unlimited messages, playlists, schedules
- Unlimited departments
- Unlimited users
- Cloud storage for your media
- Free 30-day trial (no credit card required)

There are no per-screen feature gates — every plan unlocks every feature.

## In this section

- **[Updating Billing Info](./updating-billing-info)** — change your payment method

## Managing screens (and your bill)

You're billed per **active** screen. Add or remove screens any time in **Step 3: Screens** — the next month's bill adjusts automatically.

To cancel completely: **Settings → Billing → Cancel All Subscriptions**.

## Invoices

Log in to SignPresenter, click your name in the top right, go to **Settings → Billing**, choose the month, and click **Print** to save as PDF.
