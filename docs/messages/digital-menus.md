# Make Your Own Digital Menus

<div className="article-intro">

The **Designer** is SignPresenter's built-in graphic editor. Use it to build digital menus, signage posters, event boards, or anything else — and update them whenever you like.

## Build a menu from scratch

1. Select **Step 1: Messages** and choose **Horizontal 16:9** (or Vertical, whichever fits your screen).
2. Select the **Designer** template.
3. Assign it to a **Category** and give it a **Name**.
4. Click **Launch Designer**.

### Edit your design

In the Designer:

- **Add an element** — Click **Add Element** in the top-right. Choose Text, Image, or Box.
- **Edit text** — Click any text to edit it. Customize font, color, animation, and more in the right-hand panel.
- **Edit an image** — Click an image, then **Image Options** in the right panel. Click the selected image and choose **Upload** to replace it. Crop to fit.
- **Aspect lock** — Set Lock to **No** to resize an image freely on both axes.
- **Layers** — Move elements forward or back, and add animations.
- **Duplicate** — Use **Duplicate Element** at the top of the right-hand edit box.
- **Add a new image** — **Add Element → Add Image**. SignPresenter inserts a placeholder you can replace via Upload.

When you're done, click **Save Changes** in the top-right corner. You'll return to the message edit screen — click **Save** there too to apply the design to the message.

:::tip
The **Copy** button (top-right) duplicates your current design before editing. Use it as a "save as" so you can experiment without losing your original.
:::

## Use a Designer template instead

SignPresenter ships with pre-built designer templates — useful starting points if you don't want to build from scratch.

1. Select **Step 1: Messages** and choose **Design** from the left-hand menu.
2. Click **Designer Templates** on the New Message screen.
3. Pick a template, enter a category and name, and click **Launch Designer**.
4. Edit the logos, pictures, and text — everything is customizable.
5. Click **Save Changes**, then **Save** on the message screen.

## Add the design to a playlist

1. Select **Step 2: Playlists** at the top.
2. Choose **Horizontal 16:9** and click **Create a New Custom Playlist**.
3. Name your playlist, then select your new design from the **Available Content** box on the right.
4. Click **Save**.

## Show it on a screen

1. Go to **Step 3: Screens** and select the screen you want.
2. Add the playlist, or schedule it via the **Schedules** box on the right-hand side.

That's it — your design is live.
