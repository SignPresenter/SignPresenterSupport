# Uploading Videos

<div className="article-intro">

Videos give your signage motion and personality. SignPresenter supports most common formats — but there are a couple of size and codec rules worth knowing up front.

</div>

<div className="prereqs">
<h4>Before you start</h4>

- Your video must be **under 1 GB**. If yours is bigger, compress it with [HandBrake](https://handbrake.fr/) (free).
- Use a Horizontal or Vertical message — the layout must match your screen orientation.

</div>

## Upload steps

1. Go to **Messages** in SignPresenter. Click **Horizontal** or **Vertical**, then choose **Video With Audio** or **Silent Video**.
2. Type a **Category** and a **Name** for the video.
3. Click the blue **Upload** button, find the video on your computer, and select it.

   ![Click upload](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/145209385-1bdcf19b-1d18-4518-8d7c-3ab0949a2d79.png)

4. You'll see a **Pending Upload** label. Click **Save** to start the upload.

   ![Pending upload](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/145209528-3e53bf7f-3e1c-4ceb-b8a1-aa0550752ce4.png)

5. Stay on the page until you see the next screen — this is when the actual upload happens.

   ![Upload in progress](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/145209728-2289d10e-7348-4f15-9c87-1aeb92a9de06.png)

6. Once uploaded, you'll see the message below. You can leave the page now — processing happens server-side. Come back later to confirm the video processed cleanly.

   ![Upload complete](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65249159/145209905-efad06d9-3739-4084-b01a-14a4408cbf6b.png)

## What if the video fails to process or shows up black?

We work hard to support every codec, but sometimes a video fails to process. If yours does:

1. Open it in [HandBrake](https://handbrake.fr/) (free).
2. Re-encode it (HandBrake's default preset is fine in most cases).
3. Re-upload the new file.

Changing the codec almost always fixes processing problems.

If you've tried that and it still doesn't work, email [support@signpresenter.com](mailto:support@signpresenter.com) and — if possible — share the original video via Dropbox or Google Drive. We'll analyze it and help you get the issue resolved.
