# Connect Canva to SignPresenter

<div className="article-intro">

SignPresenter pulls designs straight out of your Canva account — no exporting, no re-uploading. Connect once, then every Canva design is available as a message.

</div>

<div className="prereqs">
<h4>Before you start</h4>

- Canva integration currently supports **horizontal (16:9) screens only**. Vertical support is coming.
- Canva pulls in **images** only at this time (not videos or animations).
- Start your Canva designs at **at least 1920 × 1080 pixels** so they look sharp on TV.

</div>

![Canva setup walkthrough](/img/canva-tutorial.webp)

## Step 1 — Go to My Content

Click **Step 1: My Content** at the top of the screen, then click the green **+** button to create a new message.

![Go to My Content](/img/canva-step1.png)

## Step 2 — Select the Horizontal layout

Choose **Horizontal 16:9** for your screen layout.

![Select Horizontal Layout](/img/canva-step2.png)

## Step 3 — Select the Canva template

Click the **Canva** option in the **Popular Message Templates** section.

![Select Canva Template](/img/canva-step3.png)

## Step 4 — Select Canva Design

Click the **Select Canva Design** button to open the Canva connection screen.

![Select Canva Design](/img/canva-step4.png)

## Step 5 — Connect your Canva account

If this is your first time, you'll see a **Connect Your Canva Account** prompt. Click **Connect to Canva** — this is a one-time setup. After connecting, you can browse and select any design from your Canva library.

Give the message a **Name**, choose a **Category**, set the **Duration**, and click **Save**.

![Connect Your Canva Account](/img/canva-step5.png)

## You're done

Your Canva design is now available to add to any horizontal playlist.

:::tip
Update a design in Canva and SignPresenter pulls the new version automatically — no need to re-export or re-upload.
:::
