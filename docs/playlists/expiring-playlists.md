# Expiring Playlists

<div className="article-intro">

Use **Limit Dates** to schedule a playlist to start and stop on specific calendar dates. Great for holiday promotions, conference signage, fundraising drives, or any campaign with a defined window.

## How to use Limit Dates

1. Create the message(s) you want to time-limit.
2. Select **Step 2: Playlists** and choose **Horizontal 16:9** or **Vertical 9:16**.
3. Click **Create a New Custom Playlist**, name it, and click **Save**.
4. In the Edit Playlist screen, add your messages from **Available Content**.
5. At the top of the box, click the greyed-out **Limit Dates** button — this opens a Start Date / End Date picker.
6. Set your **Start Date** and **End Date** and click **Save**.
7. Go to **Step 3: Screens** and select the screen you want this dated playlist on.
8. In the **Schedules** box on the right, open the schedule.
9. Add the dated playlist to the schedule's playlists, and choose the **weight** (the percentage of run time the dated playlist should take).
10. Click **Save**.

The playlist will automatically start on the start date and stop on the end date — no manual cleanup needed.

:::tip
Combine Limit Dates with **weight** to gradually phase a campaign in or out: start at 20% weight, ramp to 50%, drop back to 10% in the final week.
:::
