# Playlists

<div className="article-intro">

A **playlist** is a sequence of messages. Add messages, drag them into the right order, and SignPresenter rotates through them on your screens. You can have one big playlist or many — and schedule different ones at different times of day.

</div>

## How playlists work

Every account starts with a default **Custom Playlist** containing a weather message. You can:

- **Add messages** by clicking them in the **Available Content** panel
- **Reorder** by dragging
- **Remove** with the trash icon
- **Save** when you're done

The Custom Playlist is already attached to your first screen, so anything you add starts playing right away (after a short cache update).

## Building blocks

- **Custom Playlist** — your own ordered messages
- **Slideshow** — upload a batch of images and rotate through them
- **External Feed** — pull messages from a [feed](../feeds/) you've subscribed to (Lessons.church, ChiroThinTV, etc.)
- **3-Zone Playlist** — combine three independent zones into one screen layout
- **Limit Dates** — make a playlist start and stop on specific dates
