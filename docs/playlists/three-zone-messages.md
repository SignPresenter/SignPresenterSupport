# Build a Three-Zone Display

<div className="article-intro">

Three-zone templates are a powerful way to create clean, modern signage — a main message zone, a secondary zone, and a marquee strip. With SignPresenter it takes about ten minutes to build the first one.

## Recommended dimensions

| Ratio | Pixels | Ideal for |
|-------|--------|-----------|
| 16:9 | 1920 × 1080 | Landscape full-screen |
| 9:16 | 1080 × 1920 | Vertical signage |
| 4:1 | 1280 × 320 | Marquee / message strip zones |

## Step 1: Create your 4:1 strip image

1. Go to **Step 1: Message** in the left menu.
2. Click **More → 4:1 (For Three Zone Template)**.
3. Add a **Name** and assign it to a **Category**.
4. Click **Thumbnail → Upload** and select your image.
5. Crop and **Save**.

## Step 2: Build a 4:1 playlist

1. Go to **Step 2: Playlist**.
2. Click **More → 4:1 (For Three Zone Template)**.
3. Choose **New Custom Playlist** or pick a template.
4. Name your playlist.
5. Select images from the **Available Content** area on the right.
6. Click **Save**.

## Step 3: Combine into a three-zone display

1. Back in **Step 1: Message**, choose **Horizontal** or **Vertical**.
2. Click **All Designs → Three Zone**.
3. Name the template and assign a category.
4. Choose ratios and assign content to each zone:
   - 📺 Top and bottom for horizontal layouts
   - 📱 Left and right for vertical layouts
5. Click **+ New** to assign playlists or messages to each zone.
6. Repeat for all three zones, then click **Update**.

:::tip
Want a vertical three-zone display? Select **Vertical** when creating the message and follow the same process.
:::
