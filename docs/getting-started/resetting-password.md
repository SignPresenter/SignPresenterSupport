# Resetting Your Password

<div className="article-intro">

If you can't remember your SignPresenter password, the **Forgot Password** flow emails you a one-time reset link. Total time: about a minute.

</div>

## Steps

1. Go to [signpresenter.com](https://signpresenter.com) and click **Sign In**.
2. On the sign-in page, click the **Forgot your password?** link below the **Sign In** button.
3. Enter the email address associated with your account.
4. Click **Reset Password**.

We send a reset link to the email on file. Click the link in the email and you'll be signed in automatically with a temporary session.

5. From there, open **Settings** in the top-right and update your password under your profile.

## I didn't get the email

A few things to check:

- **Wait a couple of minutes** — delivery can lag occasionally.
- **Check spam / junk** — reset emails sometimes land there.
- **Confirm the email address** — make sure you typed the exact email associated with your account. The system says "Email not found" if there's no match.
- **Different email?** Some users sign up with a personal email and forget. Try any address you might have used.

If none of those work, [open a support ticket](../troubleshooting/) or email [support@signpresenter.com](mailto:support@signpresenter.com) and we'll reset it manually.

## I'm logged in but want to change my password

You don't need the reset flow for this — go to **Settings → your profile** and update the password directly. See [Your Profile](../account/profile).
