# Inviting Users

<div className="article-intro">

SignPresenter accounts include unlimited users at no extra cost. Invite a colleague to manage messages, build playlists, or look after a specific department — and pick the permission level that fits.

</div>

## Steps

1. Click your name in the top-right and choose **Settings**.
2. In the **Departments** panel, click into the department where you want to grant the user access. (If your account only has one department, that's where you'll add them.)
3. In the **Department Permissions** box, click **New**.
4. Enter the user's email address.
5. Click **Save**.

The user receives an invitation email with a link. They click it, set a password, and log in — they'll see only the department(s) you've added them to.

## Choosing a permission level

The role you grant during invite (or update afterward in the same panel) controls what the user can see and do:

| Role | Can do |
|------|--------|
| **User** (default) | Build messages, playlists, and screens within their department. Can't change billing or feed subscriptions. |
| **Owner** | Everything a User can do, plus manage [billing](../billing/) and [feed subscriptions](../feeds/). |
| **Creator** | Everything a User can do, plus create and manage [public feeds](../admin/feeds/) (publishing-side). |
| **Admin** | Full access — including [custom templates](../admin/templates/) and the [admin dashboard](../admin/admin-dashboard). |

You can grant multiple roles to one user. Most accounts only need one or two admins.

## Adding the same user to multiple departments

If you have multiple campuses or franchises, the same person can have access to several departments:

1. Repeat the invite flow in each department.
2. The user gets one email per department they're added to.
3. Once they log in, they see a department switcher in the top-right.

## Removing a user

In the same **Department Permissions** panel, find the user, and click the trash icon next to their row. They lose access immediately. You can re-invite them later — their account isn't deleted, just disconnected from your department.

## What new users see

New users land on the **Welcome** page and walk through the same three-step flow as a brand-new account: create a message, build a playlist, pair a screen. They only see screens and playlists in the department(s) you granted them — plus anything you've explicitly **shared with all departments**.

See [Multiple Campuses & Departments](./departments) for the full sharing model.
