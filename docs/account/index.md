# Account & Departments

<div className="article-intro">

A SignPresenter **account** is your top-level container — every screen, message, and playlist lives inside one. Need separate areas for multiple campuses, franchises, or ministries? **Departments** let you carve up a single account into self-contained sub-areas, each with its own users and screens.

</div>

## Account essentials

Your $10/month-per-screen subscription includes:

- Unlimited messages, playlists, and schedules
- Unlimited users
- Unlimited departments
- Cloud storage for your media

There are no per-user or per-department fees.

## In this section

- **[Departments](./departments)** — Add multiple campuses, franchises, or ministries inside one account, share content across them, and assign users to specific locations.
