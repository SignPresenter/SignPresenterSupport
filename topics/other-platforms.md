# What Platforms Does SignPresenter Work On?

SignPresenter works on multiple platforms:

## Amazon Fire TV Devices
SignPresenter works on **Amazon FireStick** and **Fire TV** devices running **Fire OS** (Android-based).

**Recommended device:** [Fire TV Stick HD](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0CQMRKRV5) - Most affordable option with auto-reboot capability, perfect for 24/7 digital signage.

## Android Devices
SignPresenter now supports **Android devices running Android 10.0 or newer**.

Download from [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.signpresenter.screen&hl=en_US)

**Budget-friendly Android options:**
- [$19.88 onn. Android TV 4K UHD Streaming Device](https://www.walmart.com/ip/onn-Google-TV-4K-Streaming-Box-New-2023-4K-UHD-Resolution/2835618394)
- [$34.88 onn. Google TV 4K Plus Streaming Device](https://www.walmart.com/ip/ONN-4K-PLUS/15557424949)

## Fire TVs (Built-In)
TVs with built-in **Fire OS** work great for digital signage.
