## Set Up Your ChiroThin TV Account

Complete setup in under 10 minutes and start displaying professional content for your practice.

[![Set-Up Your ChiroThinTV Account](https://img.youtube.com/vi/t-BMoUwDsCE/0.jpg)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t-BMoUwDsCE)

---

## Step 1: Create Your ChiroThin TV Account

1. Go to [ChiroThinTV.com](https://chirothintv.com)
2. Click **Start My Free Trial** or **Register**
3. Fill out all necessary information
4. Log into your new account

---

## Step 2: Set Up Your Screen in Sign Presenter

1. Go to **Step 3: Screens**
2. Click **First Screen**
3. Name your screen (e.g., "Reception Area")
4. Click **Save**

---

## Step 3: Install Sign Presenter on Your Fire Stick

### Device Requirements
Any Fire Stick from 2020 or newer will work, including the [Fire TV Stick HD](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0CQMRKRV5).

### Installation Steps

1. **Set up your Fire Stick**: Plug it in, sign into your Amazon account (create a free account if needed)
2. **Search for Sign Presenter**: Hold the microphone button on your remote and say "Sign Presenter"
3. **Download the app**: Select the Sign Presenter app and click **Download**
4. **Launch the app**: Once installed, open Sign Presenter from your home screen

---

## Step 4: Pair Your Device

1. Sign Presenter will display a **4-digit code** on your TV
2. Go back to [SignPresenter.com](https://signpresenter.com) on your computer
3. Click **Pair TV** in the Screens section
4. Enter the 4-digit code
5. Click **Pair TV**
6. On your Fire Stick, click **Run Schedule**

---

## Step 5: Add Billing Information

After clicking Run Schedule, you'll be prompted to add billing:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. Enter your credit card information
3. Click **Update**

**Note:** Your billing won't start for 30 days, and you can cancel subscriptions at any time.

Your ChiroThin TV feed will now start running on your screen!

---

## Optional: Advanced Features

### Create a Manual Playlist for Patient Education

This slideshow lets you manually present pricing and information to new patients.

1. Go to **Playlists** → **Horizontal 16x9**
2. Find **ChiroThin Manual Playlist**
3. Name it (e.g., "Patient Pricing")
4. Choose your preferred pricing points
5. Click **Save**

**Using the Manual Playlist:**
1. Press the **Back** button on your Fire Stick remote
2. Select the **Manual Playlist**
3. Use the left/right arrows to navigate through slides
4. Press the **Up** arrow to see all slides at once
5. To return to the feed, press **Back** and select **Run Schedule**

---

### Add Social Media Promotion

1. Go to **Playlists** → **Horizontal 16x9**
2. Find **Social Media Promotion**
3. Click **Subscribe**
4. Click **Create Playlist**
5. Name your playlist
6. Enter your Facebook, Twitter, or Instagram handles
7. Click **Save**

---

### Add Google/Yelp Reviews Request

1. Go to **Playlists** → **Horizontal 16x9**
2. Find **Reviews**
3. Click **Subscribe**
4. Click **Create Playlist**
5. Name it "Reviews"
6. Click **Save**

This will display prompts asking viewers to leave Google or Yelp reviews.

---

### Upload Your Own Images

1. Click **Messages** → **Upload**
2. Upload your images
3. Create a category and name
4. Videos and other message types are included in your subscription

---

## Step 6: Add Playlists to Your Screen

1. Go to **Step 3: Screens**
2. Click on your screen
3. Select **24/7**
4. Adjust playlist weights (must equal 100%):
   - ChiroThin Auto Playlist: 50%
   - Reviews: 5%
   - Social Media: 5%
   - (Remaining percentage for other playlists)
5. Click **Save**

Your screen will update within 1-2 minutes to show all playlists at the correct weight.
