## How to Set Up a Vertical Screen

Setting up a vertical screen in Sign Presenter takes less than 30 seconds. Follow these simple steps:

[![Vertical screen setup](https://img.youtube.com/vi/8LE2L7zqV1I/0.jpg)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8LE2L7zqV1I)

---

### Step 1: Change Screen Rotation

1. Go to the **Screens** tab (Step 3)
2. Select **First Screen** from the left sidebar
3. Find the **Rotation** dropdown
4. Select **90 - Vertical** (or **270 - Vertical** depending on your orientation)
5. Click **Save**

### Step 2: Switch to Vertical Playlist

1. Scroll down to the **Playlist** section
2. Select your vertical playlist from the dropdown
3. Remove any horizontal playlists if needed
4. Click **Save**

### Step 3: Reload the App on Your Device

For the rotation change to take effect:

1. On your Fire Stick or Android device, press the **Home** button
2. Navigate back to the Sign Presenter app
3. Open Sign Presenter again
4. Your screen will now display in vertical mode

---

### Other Rotation Options

- **0 - Horizontal**: Standard landscape orientation
- **90 - Vertical**: Portrait mode (rotated 90° clockwise)
- **180 - Horizontal**: Upside-down landscape
- **270 - Vertical**: Portrait mode (rotated 90° counter-clockwise)

**Note:** After changing rotation settings, you must exit and reopen the Sign Presenter app for changes to take effect.
